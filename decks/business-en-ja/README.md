# Business English ↔ Japanese

A deck pack for working professionals — meetings, emails, project management, and engineering vocabulary.

This is the deck Hiroshi (the Tokyo backend engineer in [`docs/use-cases.md`](../../docs/use-cases.md)) needs. It's also useful for English-speaking professionals working with Japanese teams.

## Suggested splits

- `meetings.md` — vocabulary for running, attending, and recapping meetings
- `email.md` — formal email phrasing in both directions
- `engineering.md` — software-development terminology (deploy, rollback, postmortem, etc.)
- `project-management.md` — milestones, blockers, sprints, retros
- `keigo-basics.md` — when and how to use 敬語 in business contexts

If you want to own one of these, open a `deck:` issue.
